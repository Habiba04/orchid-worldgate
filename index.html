About Us

We are a fully integrated grower, producer and exporter of fresh, frozen (IQF) fruits and vegetables, and processed food.
We grow our crops in-house under strict quality control, then manage the freezing and processing stages internally to ensure full traceability and compliance with global standards.
Our mission is To strengthen the global reach of Egyptian products by delivering integrated export solutions that combine market intelligence, branding, supply chain expertise and strategic partnerships, building on international trade agreements and facilitation between Egypt and the world to contribute to the economic welfare and success for producers and stakeholders.Egypt’s reputation as a source of quality, authentic, and sustainable exports. -Orchid World Gate.

Services

With a strong commitment to quality and realiability. we aim to represent the best of Egyptian agricultural and food products around the world.

Export of Frozen Vegetables and Fruits

Vegetables: Broccoli, Cauliflower, Green Peas, Beans, Okra, Molokhia, Artichokes, Grilled Eggplant, Mixed Vegetables (Mexican Mix), Frozen French Fries.

Fruits: Mango, Strawberries.

Export of Fresh Vegetables and Fruits

Vegetables: Green Peas, Green Beans, Mini Cucumber, Fresh Garlic, Dry onions, Spring Onions, Baby potatoes.

Fruits: Grapes, Mango, Dates, Pomegranate, Asparagus, Strawberry, Watermelon, Melon.

Export of Processed Food Products

Pasta (various shapes and sizes)

Tomato Paste (retail and industrial concentrations)

Flour (high-grade bakery and industrial applications)

Canned Tuna (Solid and chunks, available in all standard retail and food-service sizes)

Private Label and Brand Partnerships
