Services

We aspire to lead the agricultural export industry in Egypt, earning recognition for our unwavering commitment to quality, reliability, and sustainable practices. Through innovative solutions and optimized processes, we seek to expand market opportunities and drive the growth and global impact of Egypt's agricultural sector.

Import and Export of Agricultural Products

We specialize in the international trade of agricultural products, ensuring timely and efficient import and export services. Our global network of suppliers and partners allows us to deliver fresh, high-quality agricultural goods to meet the demands of the international market.

Export of Premium Products

Pasta: We export a broad range of pasta shapes, each produced to the highest standards.

Tomato Paste: Our tomato paste is carefully crafted to preserve the authentic flavor and nutritional value of the finest tomatoes.

Flour: Our premium flour is finely milled to meet the specific requirements of baking, food production, and other industries.

Warehousing & Third-Party Logistics (3PL)

Warehousing: Our facilities are equipped with advanced inventory management systems to guarantee accuracy and efficiency.

Third-Party Logistics (3PL): We offer end-to-end support to businesses seeking to outsource their logistics requirements.

Distribution Solutions

With a robust distribution network, we ensure that all products are delivered safely, on time, and in excellent condition. Our advanced tracking systems provide real-time updates, allowing clients to monitor the status of their shipments throughout the delivery process.

Agriculture Reclamation

We specialize in transforming unproductive or degraded land into fertile, high-yield agricultural fields. With advanced soil rehabilitation techniques, modern irrigation solutions, and sustainable farming practices.
